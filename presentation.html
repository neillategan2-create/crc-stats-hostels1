What's Coming

THE ROADMAP
WHAT WE'RE BUILDING NEXT

The Hostels 1 dashboard is just the beginning. Here's what the platform grows into as we build it out.

Phase 2 · Data Collection 📝
JotForms Integration

Home cell leaders fill in a simple online form after every meeting. That submission automatically updates the Google Sheet — and the dashboard refreshes itself. No manual data entry, no chasing people for numbers.

  • Leader submits a JotForm after home cell
  • JotForm triggers automatic Google Sheets update
  • Dashboard reflects new data in real time
  • Zero manual data management for zone leaders
Phase 2 · Expansion 🗺️
Full Zone View

Right now we see Hostels 1. The same system can be replicated for every structure in the zone — giving leadership a bird's-eye view of the entire region, not just one structure.

  • Dashboard per structure (Hostels 2, 3, Campus, etc.)
  • Zone overview — aggregate stats across all structures
  • Compare performance between structures
  • Zone leadership sees everything in one place
Phase 3 · People 👥
Leader Profiles

A dedicated page for every leader — tracking their gifts, strengths, personality, and where they operate best. Built as relationships grow. Enables intentional deployment of people into the right roles.

  • Individual profile per leader — gifts & strengths
  • Updated as you meet and build relationships
  • Match leaders by gifting and leadership style
  • See who to connect, who to develop, who to elevate
Phase 3 · Accountability 🎯
Goals Tracking

Every structure gets their goals defined and loaded into the system. The dashboard then tracks actual performance against those goals in real time — so leaders always know where they stand.

  • Set attendance, growth, and home cell targets per structure
  • Progress bars showing actual vs goal in real time
  • Zone-wide goal summary for senior leadership
  • Identify who is ahead, on track, or needs support
Phase 4 · Discipleship 📖
AI-Generated Teaching Guides

Take any leadership or discipleship book — Honor's Reward by John Bevere, Lead Like It Matters by Craig Groeschel, Killing Kryptonite, All In by Mark Batterson — and use AI to turn it into a structured, ready-to-teach curriculum. Four weeks of sessions per book, designed for one-on-one or small group delivery. Overseer teaches leaders. Leaders teach members. The same material cascades through every level of the zone.

INPUT

Paste any book into Claude. The AI reads it and structures the core principles into teachable units.

OUTPUT

4-week teaching guide — weekly theme, key scriptures, discussion questions, and practical application for leaders to use.

FLOW

Overseer → Leader (one-on-one) → Member. One book. One message. Moving through the entire zone.

  • Works with any book — honour, leadership, faith, discipleship
  • Guides stored in the platform and accessible to all leaders
  • Builds a library of teaching material over time
  • Creates consistency of message across every structure and home cell
